Component View

Piezoelectric Sole Plates

3× PVDF piezoelectric film plates embedded in boot sole. Each footstep generates ~3–5V, ~1mA. Pressure converts directly to AC, then rectified to DC.

Bag Power Bank (20,000 mAh)

Slim LiFePO4 battery pack inside bag back panel. Receives charge from boots via thin 2-core wire along bag strap. USB-A + USB-C output ports.

Phone Charging Output

USB-C fast charge (18W) or Qi wireless pad inside front pocket. Phone charges continuously while bag is worn and user is walking.

Connecting Wire

Ultra-thin 1.5mm insulated copper wire runs from boot heel up trouser seam into bag strap channel. Detachable magnetic connector at boot-trouser junction.

Step 01 — Piezo Boots

Har kadam pe boot sole ka pressure piezoelectric plates pe padta hai. PVDF film deform hoti hai → AC current generate hoti hai → bridge rectifier → DC.

~3V DC / step
⚡

Step 02 — Charge Controller

Small PCB inside heel unit. Regulates voltage, prevents overcharge. Buffers micro-pulses from steps into steady charging current.

Regulated 5V
🎒

Step 03 — Bag Battery

Wire carries regulated power up to 20,000 mAh LiFePO4 pack in bag. Battery stores all energy from walking. Charges passively all day.

USB-C 18W out
📱

Step 04 — Phone Charged

USB-C ya wireless se phone charge hota hai bag se. 10,000 steps = roughly 1,000–1,500 mAh. Ek din ki walking = ek full charge.
